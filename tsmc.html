TSMC

Company Overview

TSMC is the world's leading semiconductor foundry, specializing in advanced process technology and large-scale manufacturing efficiency. The company focuses on long-term R&D investment to maintain its technological leadership.

Core Management Strategy

TSMC emphasizes process innovation, yield improvement, and close collaboration with global clients, creating strong entry barriers in the semiconductor industry.

Learning Reflection

This case shows how long-term investment and focus on core competencies can create sustainable competitive advantage.
